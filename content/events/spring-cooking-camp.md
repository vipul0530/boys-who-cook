## About This Event

Join us for our annual Spring Cooking Camp! Boys of all skill levels are invited to spend the day in the kitchen learning to prepare a full 3-course meal from scratch.

## What to Expect

- **Morning Session**: Learn the basics of kitchen safety and knife skills
- **Afternoon Session**: Prepare a full 3-course meal together
- **Celebration**: Enjoy your creations with family and friends

## What to Bring

- Comfortable clothes (no loose sleeves)
- Closed-toe shoes
- A big appetite!

All ingredients and equipment will be provided.

## Registration

Space is limited! Register early to secure your spot. Contact us at info@boyswhocook.org to sign up.
