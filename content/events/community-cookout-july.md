## Join Us for a Free Community Meal!

The boys of Boys Who Cook invite the entire community to join us for a free cookout at Riverside Park. Our participants have been training hard and are excited to show off their culinary skills!

## Menu

Our menu will feature grilled favorites, fresh sides, and homemade desserts — all prepared from scratch by our participants.

## Free & Open to All

This event is completely free and open to everyone in the community. We simply ask that you come hungry and ready to enjoy great food and great company.

## Support Our Mission

While the meal is free, we will have a donation station for those who wish to support Boys Who Cook. All donations go directly toward our programs and supplies.
