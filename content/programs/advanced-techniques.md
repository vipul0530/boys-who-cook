## About This Program

Advanced Techniques is designed for boys who have completed Culinary Basics or who already have cooking experience. This program takes a deeper dive into culinary arts, exploring more complex techniques and international cuisines.

## What You'll Learn

- **Advanced Knife Skills**: Julienne, brunoise, chiffonade, and more
- **World Cuisines**: Explore dishes from different cultures around the world
- **Baking & Pastry**: Breads, cakes, and pastries from scratch
- **Plating & Presentation**: Making food look as good as it tastes
- **Menu Development**: How to design a full menu for an event

## Requirements

Participants should have completed Culinary Basics or demonstrate equivalent cooking experience. Ages 12–18.

## What's Next?

Graduates of Advanced Techniques are invited to become mentors and helpers in our Culinary Basics classes — a fantastic leadership opportunity!
