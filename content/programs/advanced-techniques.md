## About This Presentation

The presentation would be short, age-appropriate, and designed to inspire students who have an interest in cooking and creativity. 

## What You'll Learn

* **Gender Stereotypes in Cooking:** How outdated ideas limit boys from learning life skills
* **Why Cooking Matters:** How cooking builds confidence and independence
* **The Life Skills Gap:** What boys miss when cooking isn’t encouraged
* **Boys Who Cook Solution:** Challenging stereotypes through cooking and life skills
* **Action & Change:** Steps students can take to break stereotypes and build independence
* **How to Cook a Small Meal:** Learning to make a simple meal
