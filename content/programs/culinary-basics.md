## About This Program

Culinary Basics is our flagship beginner program, designed for boys who are just starting their cooking journey. No experience is needed — just curiosity and a willingness to learn!

## What You'll Learn

- **Kitchen Safety**: How to safely use knives, stoves, and other equipment
- **Knife Skills**: Basic cutting techniques including chopping, dicing, and mincing
- **Cooking Methods**: Sautéing, boiling, baking, and grilling
- **Simple Recipes**: Breakfast, lunch, and dinner recipes you can make at home
- **Food Hygiene**: Proper food handling and cleanliness

## Who It's For

This program is open to boys ages 10–16, regardless of cooking experience. All skill levels are welcome!
